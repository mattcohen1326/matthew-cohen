Background

I grew up in Millburn, New Jersey. Growing up my main passions included playing hockey, listening to music, and solving puzzles. I always loved playing with legos and creating things. I went to Millburn High School where I took computer science classes and learned Java. During my time there I developed a game in Corona SDK and attempted to build a home made drone with a team of five. These experiences in highschool sparked my interest that would lead me to my Computer Science Major at CU Boulder.
